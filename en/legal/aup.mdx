This Acceptable Use Policy (AUP) applies to all users of ruoli ([ruoli.dev](https://ruoli.dev)). By using the ruoli service, you agree to comply with this policy.

**Effective date: June 21, 2026**

## Prohibited uses

You may not use the ruoli service for any of the following:

- **Illegal activity**: Engaging in or facilitating any violation of applicable laws and regulations
- **Harm to networks and infrastructure**: Attacking or damaging critical infrastructure, or gaining unauthorized access to computer systems and networks
- **Weapons and military**: Developing or designing weapons, or unlawful military applications
- **Violence and hate**: Inciting violence, spreading hate speech or discriminatory content
- **Privacy violations**: Unlawfully collecting, tracking, or monitoring personal information, or conducting unauthorized surveillance
- **Harm to minors**: Generating or distributing any content that harms minors
- **Psychological harm**: Generating content intended to cause psychological harm, manipulate, or encourage self-harm
- **Misinformation**: Producing or spreading misinformation at scale, or manipulating public opinion
- **Interfering with democratic processes**: Interfering with elections or other democratic procedures
- **Justice misuse**: Predictive policing, unauthorized facial recognition, and other high-risk justice applications
- **Fraud and deception**: Scams, impersonation, phishing, and other deceptive practices
- **Platform abuse**: Attempting jailbreaks, prompt injection, bypassing safety limits, or abusing the API
- **Sexual and adult content**: Generating sexual content involving minors, or adult content that violates the law

## High-risk use cases

When using ruoli output in the following scenarios, a qualified professional must review it before it is provided to end users, and you must disclose that AI was involved in generating the content:

- Legal advice
- Medical diagnosis and health decisions
- Insurance and financial advice
- Employment, housing, and other decisions affecting significant individual rights
- Academic testing and grading
- News content production

## Additional requirements for consumer-facing products

- Consumer-facing chatbots should disclose at the start of a session that the user is interacting with AI
- Products serving minors must follow the additional requirements of applicable law

## Consequences of violations

If a violation is found, ruoli may, depending on severity, issue warnings, throttle, suspend, or terminate service, and reserves the right to pursue liability under the law.
